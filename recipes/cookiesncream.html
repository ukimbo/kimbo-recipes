Cookies N Cream

What are Cookies N Cream

It's hard to resist a cookies and cream milkshake. The blended mix of milk, ice cream, and chunks of cookies is a delicious frozen treat. It's also easy to make at home and this milkshake recipe is fully customizable.

Ingredients

For 1 serving, you need...

  • 4-6 Chocolate sandwich cookies
  • 1/4 cup whole milk, more to taste
  • 1 1/2 to 2 cups of softened ice cream

How to MAKE Cookies N Cream

  1. Crush the cookies to your desired size. For the best control of the pieces, break them up by hand or use a mortar and pestle.
  2. In a blender, add the milk and the ice cream.
  3. Blend until smooth. Add more ice cream for a thicker consistency and more milk for a thinner milkshake.
  4. Add the majority of the cookies, reserving some as a topping. Pulse a few times, just enough to mix in the cookies but not chop them further.
  5. Pour the milkshake into one tall glass or two smaller glasses. Add more crushed cookies on top and a whole cookie for garnish, if desired.
